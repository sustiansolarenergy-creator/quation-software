Expert Answers for Solar in Tamil Nadu

Under the PM Surya Ghar scheme, residential customers in Tamil Nadu get: 1kW (₹30,000), 2kW (₹60,000), and 3kW+ (₹78,000). We assist with the TANGEDCO portal application and paperwork.

Yes! Solar panels work on light, not heat. During rainy days, production might drop by 60-70%, but the system will still generate power. Plus, the rain helps naturally wash away dust from the panels.

Solar is very low maintenance. In Tamil Nadu, we recommend cleaning the panels with plain water once every 15 days to remove dust and bird droppings. No professional chemicals are required.

The physical installation on your rooftop usually takes only 2 to 3 days. However, the TANGEDCO net-metering approval and meter change-over can take 3 to 4 weeks depending on the local EB office.

Our Tier-1 solar panels come with a performance warranty of 25 years. Most systems continue to produce power even after 30 years, though the efficiency may slightly decrease over time.
